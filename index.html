ABout Us

TransferhubNG is an online platform designed to connect football players and coaches with various scoutes, agents and football teams across the globe.
It comes with features such as player profiling and statistics, user authentication and validation. With other features to spices its audience with trending sports news, transfer and live score updates.

TransferhubNG was created to provide for footballers and coaches worldwide to upload their data. The aim is to provide platform that will serve as a go-to for clubs and agents to access players and coaches they are interested in all around the globe with specific player evaluation and detailed profilling.
